Scary Nights

G-Eazy
While the easy-going pop-rap cocktail of "Me, Myself
& I" landed G-Eazy his first top chart hit, his
subsequent "No Limit" with A$AP Rocky and Cardi B
solidified his hitmaker status. Just ahead of All
Hallows` Eve, the popular Bay Area rapper follows
that collaborative model on much of the seasonally
spooky Scaty Nights EP. He trades off with drip
specialist Gunna over the eerie "I Wanna Rock" beat,
while aligning with French Montana and Memphis
trap exemplar Moneybang Yo for the cocksure "Full
Time Cappers". When the rhythm flips to boom bap
and Miguel and The Game come through.
